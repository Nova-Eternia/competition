NATIONAL PARKS IN B.C.

Natural parks, also known as national parks, are protected areas
established to preserve the natural environment, biodiversity, and
cultural heritage. They provide a space for recreation, education,
and research while maintaining ecological integrity.

THE WILD

Wildlife refers to all undomesticated animals, plants, and other
organisms living in their natural habitats. These ecosystems support a diverse range of species. Observing wildlife can be a profound experience, offering glimpses into the behavior, adaptation, and interaction of animals and plants in their native environments.
